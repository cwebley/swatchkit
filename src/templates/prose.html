Details & Summary

What is a design token?

A design token is a named entity that stores a visual design attribute. Tokens are used in place of hard-coded values to ensure consistency and maintainability across a design system. They can represent colors, spacing, typography, and more.

How does fluid typography work?

Fluid typography uses clamp() to smoothly interpolate font sizes between a minimum and maximum viewport width. Instead of jumping between breakpoints, text scales proportionally with the viewport. The formula considers a minimum size, a preferred size based on viewport units, and a maximum size.
